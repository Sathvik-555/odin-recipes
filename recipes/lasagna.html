Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that is beloved worldwide. It is a type of pasta casserole known for its rich layers of ingredients, creating a delicious and hearty meal.Lasagna is characterized by its rich, savory flavor, combining the sweetness of tomatoes, the savory notes of meat, and the creamy, tangy taste of cheese. The texture is a delightful mix of tender pasta, hearty meat sauce, and gooey, melted cheese, with occasional crispy edges from the baked cheese on top.

Lasagna is a versatile dish that can be adapted to suit various tastes and dietary preferences, making it a favorite comfort food for many.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 jar (24 ounces) marinara sauce
  • 1 box (9 ounces) oven-ready lasagna noodles
  • 1 container (15 ounces) ricotta cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Add the jar of marinara sauce to the skillet and stir to combine. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese with the egg. Mix well.
  4. Place a layer of oven-ready lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  5. Sprinkle 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese over the ricotta mixture.
  6. Finish with a layer of noodles, remaining meat sauce, and sprinkle the top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
