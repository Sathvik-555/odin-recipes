Salad

Description

A salad is a dish consisting of mixed vegetables, fruits, and sometimes proteins such as meats, cheeses, nuts, or grains. It is typically served cold or at room temperature and is often dressed with a sauce or vinaigrette.

Salads can vary widely in ingredients and style, making them a versatile option for meals or side dishes.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula, or any mix of your choice)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots

Steps

  1. Wash and dry all the vegetables.
  2. Halve the cherry tomatoes, slice the cucumber and bell pepper, and thinly slice the red onion.
  3. In a large salad bowl, combine the mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, red onion, and shredded carrots.
  4. In a small bowl or jar, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar (or lemon juice), Dijon mustard, honey (or maple syrup), minced garlic, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  5. Serve immediately as a side dish or a light main course.
