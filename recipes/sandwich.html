Sandwich

Description

A sandwich is a versatile and popular food item consisting of two or more slices of bread with a filling between them. The filling can be a variety of ingredients, including meats, cheeses, vegetables, spreads, and condiments.

Sandwiches are enjoyed worldwide and are a staple in many cuisines due to their simplicity, portability, and endless customization options.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of mayonnaise or mustard
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices

Steps

  1. Lay out the two slices of bread on a clean surface. If you like, you can toast the bread lightly.
  2. Spread mayonnaise or mustard evenly on one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place the slices of ham on one slice of bread. Add the cheese slices on top of the ham.
  4. Place the other slice of bread on top, spread side down, to complete the sandwich.
  5. Cut the sandwich in half, if desired, and serve immediately.
